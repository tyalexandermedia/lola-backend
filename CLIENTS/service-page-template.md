## Content Guidelines

- **Word Count**: 2,000-2,500 words
- **Readability**: Flesch-Kincaid Grade 8-10
- **Primary Keyword**: Use 1-2 times in main content
- **LSI Keywords**: 8-12 variations throughout
- **Images**: 8-12 total (hero + gallery + process)
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 to other relevant services/locations
- **External Links**: 2-3 to authoritative sources
- **H1**: 1 only
- **H2**: 4-8 throughout
- **H3**: 12-20 for detailed sections
- **Paragraphs**: 100-150 words max before subheading
- **Lists**: Use for benefits, steps, service types
- **CTAs**: 2-3 spread throughout page (top, middle, bottom)
