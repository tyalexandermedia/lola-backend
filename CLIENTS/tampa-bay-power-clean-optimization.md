## 5.3 Content Writing Guidelines

### **SEO Best Practices**

- **Word count**: 2,000-3,000 for pillar pages, 1,500-2,500 for blog
- **Keyword density**: Primary keyword 1-2%, LSI keywords 8-15 mentions
- **Readability**: Flesch-Kincaid grade 8-10 (conversational but authoritative)
- **Structure**: H1 (1), H2 (3-5), H3 (8-15), bullet points, numbered lists
- **Internal links**: 3-5 contextual links per post, anchor text variation
- **External links**: 2-3 authoritative sources (not competitors)
- **Images**: 5-8 per 2,000-word post, all with descriptive alt text
- **CTAs**: 2-3 conversational CTAs (not salesy), form + phone + SMS

### **Tone & Voice Guidelines**

- **Authority**: Demonstrate expertise without arrogance
- **Conversational**: Use "you" and "we", avoid jargon where possible
- **Problem-solution**: Lead with pain points, then present solutions
- **Local pride**: Reference Tampa Bay communities by name, show local knowledge
- **Trust-building**: Share certifications, years experience, guarantees
- **Action-oriented**: End paragraphs with next steps (contact, learn more, schedule)
