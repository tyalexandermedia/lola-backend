# ASSETS

## Purpose

`ASSETS/` tracks owned leverage that can compound beyond any single client or campaign.

## What Belongs Here

- Domains and websites.
- Tracking numbers.
- Dashboards.
- Case studies.
- Automations.
- Content libraries and reusable reports.
- Asset status, owner, maintenance needs, and strategic value.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Secrets or private credentials.
- Raw PII.
- Client-specific notes that belong in `CLIENTS/`.
- Experimental ideas that belong in `EXPERIMENTS/`.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

Assets are what remain and compound as LOLA OS learns. They should be tracked separately from client work.
