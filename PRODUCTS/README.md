# PRODUCTS

## Purpose

`PRODUCTS/` is the future home for packaged LOLA OS product definitions, offers, workflows, and productized service logic.

## What Belongs Here

- Product and package definitions.
- Offer architecture and fulfillment maps.
- Product-specific workflow documentation.
- Product readiness checklists.
- Notes for turning internal systems into repeatable deliverables.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-specific account context.
- Shared core platform primitives.
- Raw templates or copy blocks that belong in `TEMPLATES/`.
- Operational SOPs that belong in `OPERATIONS/`.
- Secrets or customer data.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

`PRODUCTS/` helps LOLA OS turn repeatable growth systems into clear, sellable, and fulfillable products without mixing product strategy into client folders.
