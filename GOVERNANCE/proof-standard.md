# LOLA OS Proof Standard

LOLA OS compounds only when it separates evidence from opinion.

## Claim Status

- `assumption`: Believed but not tested.
- `hypothesis`: Structured idea ready for an experiment.
- `tested`: Tried in at least one real context with recorded evidence.
- `proven`: Repeated across enough contexts to become blueprint-level guidance.
- `deprecated`: Previously used but no longer recommended.

## Promotion Rules

- Assumptions cannot enter blueprints as rules.
- Hypotheses belong in `EXPERIMENTS/`.
- Tested learnings may enter client playbooks or blueprint candidates.
- Proven learnings may enter blueprints.
- Deprecated learnings should remain visible with the reason they were retired.

## Required Evidence Fields

Each reusable intelligence entry should eventually include:

- Source
- Date
- Client or context
- Evidence
- Metric impacted
- Confidence score
- Reusability score
- Recommended use
- Review date

## Guardrails

- Do not publish or encode fake rankings, review counts, GBP metrics, revenue numbers, or client proof.
- Do not mix client-specific proof into global blueprint rules.
- Do not store secrets in client, blueprint, pattern, or asset files.
