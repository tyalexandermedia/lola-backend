# GOVERNANCE

## Purpose

`GOVERNANCE/` defines the rules that keep LOLA OS honest, versioned, privacy-safe, and operationally usable.

## What Belongs Here

- Proof standards.
- Versioning standards.
- Promotion rules for experiments and blueprints.
- Data privacy and client-separation rules.
- Deprecation standards.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-specific implementation notes.
- Product marketing copy.
- Raw data, secrets, or credentials.
- Experimental ideas without a governance decision.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

Governance prevents assumptions from being promoted into reusable systems too early.
