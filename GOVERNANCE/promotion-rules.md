# LOLA OS Promotion Rules

## Experiment To Intelligence

An experiment can become an intelligence entry when it has:

- A documented hypothesis.
- A client or test context.
- A result.
- Evidence.
- A clear confidence level.

## Intelligence To Blueprint

An intelligence entry can become blueprint guidance when it is:

- At least `tested`.
- Relevant beyond one client.
- Free of private client data.
- Clear about when to use it and when not to use it.

## Blueprint To Product

Blueprint behavior can become productized when:

- Delivery steps are repeatable.
- Required inputs are known.
- QA checks exist.
- The proof standard is clear.
