# BLUEPRINTS

## Purpose

`BLUEPRINTS/` contains reusable industry/category systems that inherit from business-model patterns and can generate client-specific LOLA OS deployments.

## What Belongs Here

- Industry-specific SEO architecture.
- Service hierarchy and conversion structure.
- Client generation requirements.
- Reporting and tracking requirements.
- Versioned playbooks, templates, evidence, and launch checklists.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Private client data.
- One-off page copy that is not reusable.
- Untested experiments that belong in `EXPERIMENTS/`.
- Product pricing/package details that belong in `PRODUCTS/`.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

Blueprints turn proven knowledge into repeatable systems. Clients inherit from blueprints; blueprints inherit from patterns.
