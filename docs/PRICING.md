## Positioning

LOLA OS is **not** an SEO service. It is a **business growth operating system** sold as a
**phased roadmap**: clients don't buy random SEO tasks — they enter a 30 / 60 / 90+ day
visibility and revenue roadmap and advance through stages of maturity.

Core narrative:

- *"Most businesses don't have a marketing problem first. They have a foundation problem."*
- The first **30 days** create the base. Days **31–90** build signals. After **90 days** the data compounds.
- You're not paying more each month for no reason — **you're paying for maturity.** As the roadmap advances, the work expands and the data becomes more useful.
- The dashboard is the **Growth Score** — every client sees exactly where they are and what's next.

### Voice rules (hard constraints)

- Use **roadmap / foundation / growth / scale / evidence** language.
- **Never** fabricate guarantees, rankings, revenue, leads, or performance claims. **Never** promise results.
- Dashboard metrics are described as **"available when connected"** / **"where access exists"** / **"tracked as integrations are added."** Don't imply every metric is live for every client on day one.

## Core roadmap (the offer ladder)

### Phase 1 — Foundation Sprint
- **Price:** `$297` one-time (or first 30 days)
- **For:** businesses that need their online foundation created, cleaned up, or rebuilt.
- **Goal:** make the business searchable, trackable, and presentable — and deliver the *"they actually know my business"* moment.
- **Includes:** landing page / website foundation · core SEO setup · technical cleanup where possible · Google indexing basics · basic on-page SEO · primary service positioning · basic local service-area targeting · contact/call CTA setup · analytics/tracking check · dashboard access (where available) · first roadmap snapshot + visibility score + next priority.
- **Outcome:** a usable online foundation. This is the start line, not market dominance.
- **CTA:** "Start with the Foundation Sprint"

### Phase 2 — Growth Roadmap
- **Price:** `$497`/month (range `$397–$497`; **default $497**)
- **For:** businesses ready to build momentum once the foundation is in place.
- **Goal:** expand visibility, create proof, begin tracking meaningful movement.
- **Includes:** ongoing SEO improvements · landing page enhancements · service/area content expansion · GBP posting/updates (where access exists) · call/form/message tracking review · monthly roadmap update · dashboard progress tracking · basic local competitor monitoring · review strategy support · early evidence capture.
- **Outcome:** consistent visibility signals; this is where rankings, impressions, calls, and lead signals start becoming visible.
- **CTA:** "Continue into Growth"

### Phase 3 — Scale System
- **Price:** `$697`/month standard · `$997+`/month competitive (multi-location, competitive markets, heavier execution). **Default $697.**
- **For:** businesses ready to compete across multiple services, areas, and channels.
- **Goal:** turn foundation + growth work into a repeatable lead-generation system.
- **Includes:** multi-service SEO expansion · multi-city/service-area strategy · content strategy · review growth system · advanced dashboard reporting · revenue/call/lead attribution · conversion optimization · competitor monitoring · monthly strategy review · opportunity recommendations · Evidence Engine entries · blueprint feedback loop. Optional paid add-ons attach here.
- **Outcome:** an ongoing growth operating system — not just a website. 90+ days gives better data and stronger decisions.
- **CTA:** "Scale with LOLA OS"
