# KNOWLEDGE

## Purpose

`KNOWLEDGE/` is the future home for reusable knowledge that powers LOLA OS decisions, prompts, audits, reporting, and client strategy.

## What Belongs Here

- Industry research and positioning notes.
- SEO, local search, AI visibility, and growth playbooks.
- Reusable domain knowledge for service businesses.
- Methodology docs and decision frameworks.
- Non-client-specific reference material.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-owned private data.
- Live production code.
- One-off client notes that belong under `CLIENTS/`.
- Templates intended for direct reuse by products or reports.
- Secrets or proprietary credentials.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

`KNOWLEDGE/` gives agents and operators a trusted library of reusable context so client work stays consistent, grounded, and faster to execute.
