# CEO

## Purpose

`CEO/` is the future home for executive-level planning, strategic priorities, revenue goals, decision logs, and leadership-facing operating context for LOLA OS.

## What Belongs Here

- Strategic plans and company priorities.
- Decision logs and executive summaries.
- Revenue targets and business model notes.
- High-level roadmap planning.
- Founder/CEO operating context.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-specific implementation files.
- Live backend or frontend code.
- Reusable templates.
- Day-to-day support runbooks.
- Secrets, credentials, or private customer data.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

`CEO/` keeps strategic context separate from implementation details so LOLA OS can grow with clear priorities, intentional tradeoffs, and a visible decision history.
