# EXPERIMENTS

## Purpose

`EXPERIMENTS/` is where unproven ideas live before they become reusable LOLA OS knowledge or blueprint changes.

## What Belongs Here

- Hypotheses.
- Test plans.
- Results and evidence.
- Promotion or deprecation decisions.
- Links to affected clients, blueprints, products, or assets.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Proven blueprint rules.
- Client secrets or raw private data.
- Production code.
- Permanent SOPs that belong in `OPERATIONS/`.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

Experiments protect blueprints from untested ideas. Nothing should become reusable architecture until it has evidence.
