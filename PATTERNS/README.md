# PATTERNS

## Purpose

`PATTERNS/` defines reusable business-model architecture for LOLA OS. A pattern explains how a type of business works before it is adapted to a specific industry.

## What Belongs Here

- Business model definitions.
- Customer journeys and offer ladders.
- Acquisition, retention, and distribution strategies.
- Common failure modes and key metrics.
- Pattern-level assumptions and evidence requirements.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-specific facts, proof, or credentials.
- Industry-specific build instructions that belong in `BLUEPRINTS/`.
- Product packaging details that belong in `PRODUCTS/`.
- Experimental ideas that have not passed through `EXPERIMENTS/`.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

Patterns let LOLA OS generate repeatable systems across industries without starting from zero for every client.
