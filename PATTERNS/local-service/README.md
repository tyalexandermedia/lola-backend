# Local Service Pattern

## Purpose

The local service pattern defines the reusable model for businesses that sell services to a local market, such as exterior cleaning, roofing, HVAC, landscaping, and similar categories.

## What Belongs Here

- Local service customer journey.
- Local SEO and AI visibility requirements.
- Call, lead, estimate, job, and revenue tracking expectations.
- Common service-area and conversion patterns.
- Failure modes such as weak tracking, thin city pages, or unverified proof claims.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Pressure-washing-specific SEO architecture.
- Sandbar or Tampa Bay Power Clean client details.
- Private client data or secrets.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

This pattern is inherited by the pressure washing blueprint and future local-service blueprints.
