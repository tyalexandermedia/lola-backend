# OPERATIONS

## Purpose

`OPERATIONS/` is the future home for internal operating procedures, deployment checklists, QA routines, support processes, and recurring maintenance workflows.

## What Belongs Here

- SOPs and runbooks.
- Release and deployment checklists.
- QA and verification procedures.
- Incident response notes.
- Recurring reporting, billing, and client-success workflows.

## What Should Not Belong Here

- Client-specific strategy or account notes.
- Shared platform source code.
- Marketing templates.
- Product definitions.
- Secrets, tokens, or private credentials.

## How It Supports LOLA OS

`OPERATIONS/` makes LOLA OS easier to run as a real operating system: repeatable, inspectable, and resilient across clients and releases.
