Why your roof streaks

The black stains on Pinellas County roofs are alive

They're called Gloeocapsa magma — an airborne algae that lands on your roof, feeds on the limestone filler inside asphalt shingles, and spreads through Florida's humid air from roof to roof. That's why once one house on the street streaks, the neighbors follow.

The algae doesn't just look bad. It holds moisture against the roof surface, keeps shingles hotter, and slowly digests them. Left alone, it can take years off a roof's expected life — and in Dunedin, Clearwater, and Palm Harbor's climate, it never stops on its own.

Roof cleaning done right isn't cosmetic. It's maintenance that delays a five-figure replacement.

Soft wash vs. pressure washing

Why we never blast a roof with high pressure

High-pressure washing strips the protective granules off shingles, forces water under tile, and can void your roofing manufacturer's warranty. That's why shingle manufacturers and the roofing industry recommend low-pressure chemical cleaning — soft washing — as the safe method.

Our soft wash applies a biodegradable cleaning solution at lower pressure than heavy rain. It kills the algae, lichen, and mold at the root rather than shaving off the visible layer — which is why a soft-washed roof typically stays clean 18–36 months, several times longer than a pressure-washed one.

Good questions

Roof cleaning questions, answered

How much does roof cleaning cost in Dunedin?

Every roof is quoted individually based on size, pitch, material, and buildup — send your address and you'll have a clear number within 24 hours, no home visit needed. Whatever the number, it's a small fraction of the $15,000–$30,000+ a Pinellas County roof replacement runs.

Will soft washing damage my shingles or void my warranty?

No — it's the opposite. Low-pressure soft washing is the cleaning method roofing manufacturers recommend. It's high-pressure washing that strips protective granules and can void shingle warranties.

How long does a roof cleaning take?

Most residential roofs in Pinellas County are done in a few hours, in a single visit. You don't need to be home — we just need access to outdoor water and gates unlocked.

How long will my roof stay clean?

Typically 18–36 months in Tampa Bay humidity. North-facing slopes and roofs under heavy tree cover regrow faster. Ask about maintenance scheduling to stay ahead of it.

Is the cleaning solution safe for my plants and pets?

Yes. We use biodegradable solutions, pre-wet and rinse landscaping around the house, and ask that pets stay clear of treated areas while surfaces dry.

My HOA says my roof must be cleaned. What now?

Send us the notice — it's one of our most common calls in Dunedin, Palm Harbor, and Clearwater. We schedule quickly and give you finished photos to send back to the association.
