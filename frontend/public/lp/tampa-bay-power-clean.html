Why those black streaks matter

Algae isn't a stain. It's a tenant that doesn't pay rent.

The dark streaks on Pinellas County roofs are Gloeocapsa magma — a living algae that feeds on your shingles' limestone filler, holds moisture against the surface, and spreads roof-to-roof through humid air. Left alone, it can take years off a roof's life.

Blasting it with high pressure strips shingle granules and can void the manufacturer's warranty. Our soft wash uses gentle pressure and biodegradable chemistry to kill the growth at the root — so it stays gone several times longer.

How much does roof cleaning cost in Dunedin and Pinellas County?

Every roof is quoted individually based on size, pitch, material, and buildup. Send the quote form and you'll have a clear number within 24 hours — no home visit required. For context: professionally cleaning a roof costs a small fraction of the $15,000–$30,000+ a Pinellas County roof replacement typically runs.

What are the black streaks on my roof?

That's Gloeocapsa magma — an airborne algae that feeds on the limestone in shingles and thrives in Florida humidity. It holds moisture against your roof and shortens its life. Soft washing kills it at the root instead of just rinsing the stain.

Is soft washing safe for my roof, plants, and pets?

Yes. Soft washing uses very low pressure — gentler than heavy rain — with biodegradable solutions. We pre-wet and rinse landscaping and keep pets clear while surfaces dry. It's the method roofing manufacturers recommend, because high pressure strips granules and can void warranties.

My HOA sent a notice about my roof or driveway. Can you help?

This is one of our most common calls in Dunedin, Palm Harbor, and Clearwater deed-restricted communities. We schedule quickly, clean the surfaces named in the notice, and give you photos of the finished work to send back to the association.

How long will my roof or house stay clean?

In Tampa Bay humidity, a soft-washed roof typically stays clean 18–36 months and a house exterior 12–24 months, depending on shade and tree cover. Ask about maintenance scheduling to keep surfaces clean year-round.

Why seal pavers after cleaning?

Cleaning restores the look; sealing keeps it. A UV-stable sealer locks in color, stabilizes joint sand, and resists stains, weeds, and mold regrowth. Cleaning without sealing usually means the driveway looks dirty again within months.
